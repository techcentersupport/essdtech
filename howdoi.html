How Do I? Knowledge Base

Accounts & Passwords

🔑 How do I change my network password?

  1. Option A (Online): Go to office.com, log in with your school credentials, click your profile icon in the top right, and select View account → Change password.
  2. Option B (Windows Computer): Log into any district Windows PC, press Ctrl + Alt + Delete, and select Change a password.
  3. If you are completely locked out or forgot your current password, contact your school technician or submit a Help Desk ticket.
Software & Apps

📄 How do I log into Microsoft Word / Office 365?

  1. Open Microsoft Word or navigate to office.com.
  2. Click Sign In in the top right corner.
  3. Enter your full school email address (e.g., user@essd40.org).
  4. Use your standard district password to complete authentication.
Network & Wi-Fi

📶 How do I connect my device to Wi-Fi?

  1. School Devices: Connect to TIGERS (connects automatically).
  2. Personal Devices: Connect to ESSD-Public (no password required).
  3. Visit our Wi-Fi & Security page to download the required Securly SSL Certificate for personal web access.
Hardware & Printing

🖨️ How do I add or print to a district printer?

  1. Ensure you are connected to the district network.
  2. When printing a document, select one of the central follow-me print queues:
    • ESSD_FollowMe_Black (for standard black & white jobs)
    • ESSD_FollowMe_Color (for color print jobs)
  3. Walk up to any district copier, scan your badge or log in, and release your print job.
Software & Apps

🎫 How do I submit a Help Desk ticket?

  1. Visit the official ESSD Help Desk Portal.
  2. Sign in using your district Google account.
  3. Click New Ticket, describe the issue, and select your school location.
Hardware & Printing

💻 How do I fix a frozen Chromebook?

  1. Perform a hard reset by holding the Refresh key ↻ and pressing the Power key ⏻.
  2. Release Refresh when the Chromebook starts up.
  3. If the issue persists, bring the device to your building technician.
